Welcome to VAJRAMI Wedding Management Company

Our Journey

Mr. Mayjohn P J, also known as Mayjohn Findjoy, is the CEO and founder of VAJRAMI Wedding Management Company. Under his leadership, the company has achieved significant milestones in the management industry...

From the inception of VAJRAMI in 2008 in Thrissur, the vision has always been to create exceptional event experiences. Over the years, VAJRAMI has expanded to multiple cities in Kerala, catering to diverse wedding needs.

Our Vision

At VAJRAMI Wedding Management, our vision is to become Kerala's leading event management company, dedicated to crafting unique experiences for the Malayali community and people who come to the state for special occasions.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide professional wedding planning and management services throughout Kerala, delivering memorable and stress-free experiences for our clients.

A Recall of Achievements

Our journey of accomplishments has been nothing short of remarkable. VAJRAMI has excelled in wedding planning, working with esteemed clients and contributing to society.
